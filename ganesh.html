Ganesh pomegranate

Ganesh pomegranate is a popular variety known for its prolific bearing, large, yellowish-red fruits with pinkish arils and soft seeds.
It is a commercially cultivated variety in Maharashtra, India. The fruits are typically medium-sized and known for their sweet and agreeable taste.

Characteristics

  • Prolific bearer: Produces a large number of fruits.
  • Large fruit size: Fruits are generally large and round.
  • Yellowish-red rind: The skin of the fruit is a mix of yellow and red.
  • Pinkish arils: The juicy seeds inside the fruit are pinkish in color.
